Terraform: Apply

Provision & Modify Infrastructure

What is terraform apply?

The terraform apply command executes the actions proposed in the plan. It provisions, updates, or deletes resources to match your desired configuration.

Usage

After reviewing the plan, run terraform apply to implement the changes. You can also apply a saved plan file.
